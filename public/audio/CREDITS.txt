Ambient audio samples for Cosmic Chord Synth
==============================================

All three samples are licensed under CC0 1.0 Universal (Public Domain
Dedication). No attribution required, but credit is given here as a courtesy.

Source repository: https://github.com/EternityForest/Free-SFX

space-ambient.opus
  Original: Owlish/Sound Effects Pack/Scifi/blackhole2.opus
  Character: Deep cosmic drone, suitable as background for space theme.

jungle-ambient.opus
  Original: LokiF/Swamp/swamp.opus
  Character: Full layered jungle/swamp mix with insect chorus and
  creature vocalizations. Note: the source CC0 library has no true
  primate/monkey samples, so this is the closest available match.

sea-ambient.opus
  Original: rubberduck/WaterSplashSlime/loop_bubbles_02.opus
  Character: Underwater bubble loop — watery, playful, suits the sea theme.

License: Creative Commons CC0 1.0 Universal
         https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

These files can be swapped for any CC0 / public-domain alternative by
replacing the files in this directory; the engine only references them by
the filenames declared in src/components/cosmic-synth/constants.ts.
